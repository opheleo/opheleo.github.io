Who then is this?

“Who then is this, that He commands even the winds and the water, and they obey Him?” - The Gospel according to Luke, chapter 8, verses 22-25 The disciples were terrified, because it sure is terrifying to be stuck in a storm. They were terrified though they had Jesus in the boat with them because they did not realize who He really was. And so God used the storm as a means to reveal His Son to them.
