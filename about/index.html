About

Opheleo is a Greek word which means to assist, to be useful or advantageous, to profit. This online magazine with its collection of articles and essays attempts to live up to its name by being useful and profitable in the spiritual sense. Most of what is on this site is written from a Christian worldview, and the hope of the author who goes by the pen name Iraxel, is that it will assist people of all hues who stumble onto this site.

Whether you are a believer or a seeker or a disinterested passerby, may you understand more clearly the purpose of the One who taught long ago about what is truly profitable when He asked: What does it profit a man if he gains the world but loses his soul?
