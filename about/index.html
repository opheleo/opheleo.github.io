About

Writing is like meditation — the more one writes, the more one understands. And so these pages, though a constant work in progress, are attempts at writing and understanding.

Because editing too is like meditation, these pages are dynamic, in the sense that they are constantly being edited in the pursuit of more meditation and more understanding.  And the more one understands, the more one edits. So, in the continuous journey towards the essence of expression and beauty, they are constantly being edited and refined.

Lastly, and perhaps this should have been first, these pages exist here on this website because they aim to share the understanding gained. Here they stand in their digital impermanence until they are taken down or until they are blown away with the breeze, aiming to help in some little way, any passing traveller on the journey of life. Without you, the reader, they lose meaning, and they lose purpose.

So welcome to these random observations, essays, short stories, and poetry, on life, the scriptures, the news, and several other things.

Do reach out to me with bouquets, brickbats, or corrections by using the contact form here.
