About

All humans when seen in the light of eternity and infinity are like worms. Worm Magazine is for all such worms, revolving around aspects of The Word, The Son of God who became a Worm, all for worms like us. The verses from the Holy Bible illustrate this grand exchange of a Worm for worms.

How then can man be in the right before God?

How can he who is born of woman be pure?

Behold, even the moon is not bright,

and the stars are not pure in his eyes;

how much less man, who is a maggot,

and the son of man, who is a worm!”

- The Book of Job, Chapter 25, Verses 4-6

But I am a worm and not a man,

scorned by mankind and despised by the people.

- The Book of Psalms, Chapter 22, Verse 6

Fear not, you worm Jacob,

you men of Israel!

I am the one who helps you, declares the Lord;

your Redeemer is the Holy One of Israel.

- The Book of Isaiah, Chapter 41, Verse 14
